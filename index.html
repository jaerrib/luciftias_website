New EP-length single!

Kammermaschine-27.2

Kammermaschine-27.2 is now available on Bandcamp, Funkwhale, and Archive.org. This is the second entry in a four-part exploration of the space where computer science, alchemy, and the occult converge. Filtered through ambient drone and shaped by the stark geometry of German Expressionist film, it represents albedo, the purification and revelation of hidden essence. The first release in the series, Kammermaschine-27.1, is available on Bandcamp, Funkwhale, and Archive.org.
